Programs

We offer classes for every level, and each class is capped at 8-18 people so everyone can get the individual attention they need. All classes are first-come, first-served, so please secure your spot when visiting our locations.

Kick Boxing

This class combines cardio kickboxing, bag training and strength training to give you a total body workout.

Kettlebells

learn how to lift Kettlebells and your body weight safely and effectively.

Spin

Indoor cycling is a cardiovascular, butt-kicking workout that takes us on a stationary but sweaty ride of our life.

Zumba

Zumba is an aerobic fitness program featuring movements inspired by various styles of Latin American dance.

Cardio Fuse

Cardio Fuse blends elements of aerobics, kickboxing, plyometrics, and resistance training, and core work for a full body workout.

Yoga

Stretch, breathe, and relax. This class will not only help you increase your flexibility, but more importantly, let you take a break from the stress of life.
